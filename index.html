IDAM — Identity & Access Management

Provisioning, deprovisioning, role changes, and access requests. Follows NIST SP 1800-2 lifecycle stages.

IDAM Ticket Lifecycle · Click to expand reference guide
▼
1
Identity Request
User/system submits access request. HR/manager approval required. Identity proofing gathered.
2
Provisioning
Digital identity created in AD/IdP. Credentials issued. Least-privilege applied.
3
Access Assignment
RBAC configured. SSO/MFA enrolled. Resources mapped per job function.
4
Work Performed
Technical implementation documented. Evidence collected.
5
Verify & Close
Ticket reviewed, work doc attached, closed. Audit trail complete.

ServiceNow — Tickets & Active Directory

Incident management, AD on-prem issues, change requests, and service catalog items.

ServiceNow Lifecycle & AD Reference · Click to expand
▼
1
Ticket Logged
Incident/Request raised. Categorized and SNOW ticket ID assigned.
2
Triage & Assign
Priority assessed (P1-P4). Assigned to team. SLA clock starts.
3
Investigation
Root cause analysis. AD logs reviewed, replication checked, GPO investigated.
4
Resolution
Fix applied. AD object corrected. Changes tracked in SNOW change record.
5
Close & PIR
Ticket closed. Post-incident review for P1/P2. KB article created if new issue.
